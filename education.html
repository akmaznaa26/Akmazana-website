Kindergarten

I started my kindergarten journey in 2012 at Pra Ibnu Khaldun, Sekolah Kota Jembal, where I studied for one year. I truly cherish all the beautiful memories I created there with my friends. One of my favourite moments was spending time at the playground in front of our classroom together with my classmates. I also really loved the teachers who taught me because they were kind and caring. Until today, I still remember them and the happy moments I experienced during my kindergarten days 🌸🎀

Primary School

This photo was taken when I was in Year 4, and my class name was 4 Pintar. I still remember that there were around 40 students in this class. I was not really close to everyone, especially the boys, but I still had a few close friends who always went to the canteen, toilet, and many other places together with me. I have not met most of them since we finished UPSR, but I still remember the memories we shared together ✨💖

Secondary School

This photo was taken when I was in Form 5 Ibnu Sina. Most of them had been my classmates since Form 1, so we shared many memories and struggles together, especially while preparing for SPM. I still remember going to extra classes every Friday and Saturday, and buying food from the "pasar pagi" near our school during break time. Our teachers were also very friendly, which made learning less stressful and more fun 🥰💞

University

This is my classmates when I was in Semester 1. Even though we were only classmates for one semester because our class was disbanded, I really enjoyed being with them because they were very friendly, caring, and always helped each other with assignments. From Semester 2 until Semester 4, I am now in Class E, and the vibe feels almost the same as my previous class, which makes me feel comfortable because they never treat us like strangers. Sadly, we do not have a proper photo together to put here, but I truly appreciate and love all my friends in university, whether they are my classmates, roommates, or band members 🥺🎀
